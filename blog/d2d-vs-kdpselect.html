Draft2Digital vs KDP Select

Published 2026-03-01

Wide vs exclusive

KDP Select requires exclusivity. Draft2Digital supports wide distribution across many stores and libraries.

When Select helps

If your audience is mostly on Amazon and you want KU page-reads, Select can help.

When wide is better

If you want global storefront presence and library reach, go wide.
